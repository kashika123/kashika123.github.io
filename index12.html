Project Jubatus Ebergy Drink



Category: Packaging Design

Client:Atheletes Unisex

Project date: 2020

Jubatus mean Cheetah in Latin which is a Specie. This name has selected because of speed and power. Thus,this jubatus energy drink has been invented for atheletes who adults of both gender because they are often practiced outside in the sun.

As speed is the important factor to my target audience, hence yellow is being the principal colour for this brand. It is also because its connection to the sun because of the outdoor sports most of the times for the atheletes. Yellow often catches people’s attention and forces people to pause and reflect.
