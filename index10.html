My Local Services — Recycle Smart

My Local Services — Recycle Smart Feature



Category: UI | UX Design

Client: Adults, Children

Project date: 2020

The individual project is based on this mobile app named “My Local Services” in which we were required to add a new A new recycling feature, for our existing target audience which is families with small children to provide them with creative ideas as to how certain items can be recycled for home and everyday use where the users will feel confident that they are helping to create a longer-term future, while at the same time exercising their creative skills.

Hence, before designing the new feature it is important to analyse the consistency, Visibility, Match between system and the real world of the interface in terms of the typography, content, colour, User control, design process and it’s Effective Interaction Points.

When an arrow on the right side of the page is clicked, it opens a new interface, as shown in the image. The “waste” and “push” functions also provide the user with “push notifications” the night before the collection and activate lightning, which is displayed in the user’s lock screen as a reminder the next morning.

The right side arrow and the push notification button represent the universal design principle of informing users that the application is operational.




SOLUTIONS

TO be able to come up with the new feature, an icon is required to design to implement in the app for the users to know that this icon will meet their expectations to be able to recycle items in a new creative way in their daily life and also be able to make money through it or even buy items which are posted for recycle at cheap price which might be useful to others.

The first thing to be able to do mnemonics , it is important to create an icon for the new feature which will meet the expectations of the users and for this a mnemonic on the topic is needed to come up with a new icon and a name of it.

As a result, the icon is called Renovate, which is another word for “recycling.” Renovate was chosen because it sounds new and modern, similar to recycling old things into new things.

The “Renovate” button icon the users will click, it will be opened directly to that specific interface, with other options such as camera scan, DIY videos and DIY images, trash games for the people to be able to develop their creative skills according to the specific product they want to renovate by themselves

Aesthetic and minimalist design

As we can see, the entire application interface is a red theme with just six main characteristics at the bottom, which is very minimal. No useless feature or button is present in the ui.
