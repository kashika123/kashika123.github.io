Category: Packaging Design

OVA is an organic and hygienic brand that believes that the success of your product does not come at the expense of your well-being.
The OVA hygienic brand strives to ensure the comfort and well-being of its customers.The brand’s name is derived from the word “ovary,” which refers to an egg inside a woman’s body.
By putting the best of nature and delivering good for you for everyday women, the business is able to articulate its concept and ideology.

V= Vagina + Drop of Absorption + Spiral as Pattern = Menstruation cycle /Innoncence/growth/evolution/Purity

However, the drop of absorption icon is used in the logo in Alphabet V because V represent the vagina for which those products will be used and the drop of absorption icon is in white because white represents purity which represents also the drop of menstrual blood, drop of oil, drop of vagina wash product.

The Sacred Spiral – The spiral is a universal sign of growth and evolution. Infinity and continuity are represented by the spiral. The spiral appears in nature daily. It’s a symbol of innocence, rebirth, and eternal life. The sacred spiral is a light symbolizing energetic sign.

Hence, the spiral pattern is used to represent the ovarian cycle, menstruation cycle which a period of purification and growth

White and fushia are the principal colors of the logo.
These colors enhance and highlight the feminine attraction of the brand.







PACKAGING DESIGN PRODUCT BOXES and BAG

To maintain the branding’s look and feel, everything is designed in the same way. Sanitary items like sanitary napkins, oil moisturizers, mini sanitary napkins, tampons, and wipes are all packaged in black, dark purple and light nude colour with some additional text in white and black.


This minimalist approach emphasizes the goods’ purity, demonstrating that you don’t need flashy exteriors to sell what’s nice on the inside; the products will speak for themselves.


The logo and pattern are put on the sanitary wrappers, which are also kept simple. The sanitary wrappers themselves have a clean and traditional appearance, thanks to the lightness of the color palette

Under the logo there is a slogan that addresses the identity of the brand. This enables OVA to preserve its brand name by using a primary label to preserve the versatility of product design.

THE PROBLEM AND SOLUTION

The patterns mostly used are butterflys, wings, flowers for every feminine hygiene brand product and even posters were too descriptive.

SOLUTION

Hence, I came up with an original icon of Spiral to be able to use as pattern in my packagings and the droplets icon for my poster to create a new essence and fresh feminine feeling because many old posters in which the use of sanitary pad was created in different art direction to promote the sanitary napkin and it has become too common.
