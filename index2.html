PUBLICATIONS

Book

To preserve a professional and business appearance, the book incorporates the brand's characteristic colour palette. The usage of pattern on the cover and back cover design promotes the brand's welcoming tone of voice and also creates a visually appealing book that will entice visitors to examine the booklet in order to learn more about the brand's services and products. The inside page body content has no effect on readability and aids potential readers in comprehending the facts about the brand's offerings. The booklet raises awareness of the housing estate's amenities, ambience, and specifications, which is beneficial for the target audience to understand the benefits and characteristics.

A4 FLYER

The flyer is used as promotional touchpoints for the brand to reach a bigger target market and also increase the purchase of properties. The colours used on the flyer is consistent to the brand to maintain the corporate look. All elements in the flyer are aligned to a grid system which makes the flyer visually appealing and neat.








BILLLBOARD

The billboard design is vibrant and uncomplicated, reflecting the brand's personality. For the audience, the design was made with a warm tone of speech. The billboard follows a visual hierarchy, with the estate's emblem being the most crucial because it is the first thing people see when they see the brand. Second, the slogan, photos, and contact information provide information to the audience, and they learn about the brand's offerings.




SALE SIGN BOARD

The information about the qualities of the residences is included on the property sale signs, as well as contact information for possible clients who want to learn more. The selling notice also includes images of the villa's exterior and inside. The colour palette chosen expresses the brand's identity while still maintaining visual consistency.

The Website - Desktop Version

The website landing page acts as an entry point for customers and i must immediately make a good impression of the brand. The website makes use of the same tone of voice and colour palette to maintain the corporate style and also supports the brand personality.


The design of the website version is simple to use and avoids information overload. The real estate  maintains its status  on the internet so that visitors are aware of the villa's interior and exterior as well as its amenities. This is to make price and readability online simple.







THE Mobile Design Version


The website design of the mobile version contains the same information as desktop version and responsive also because many people uses their mobile also to have access to anything and the design is build in a simple and direct path so that users do not get confused and able to search for what they want to know about GreeNaze villa.





Hence, the entire branding for the housing estate is powerful because it maintains consistency in colors, personality, and tone of voice throughout all marketing collaterals such as brochures, flyers, and billboards, among other things. This company adheres to its vision statement of caring for consumers' requirements, which elevates the brand's professionalism and distinguishes it from its competitors. The brand has a well-thought-out design approach based on extensive research into rivals, target audiences, and current assets. All of this research has aided in developing the brand's identity and distinguishing it from its competitors.

THE PROBLEM AND SOLUITON



GreeNaze’s brand is totally dependent on photography, which is used to promote and market their property to clients through mediums such as billboards, sale signs, flyers, magazines, and the company’s website.

As you can see in the final deliverables, there is a lot of imagery that features GreeNaze’s surroundings, architecture, and interior items. To attract visitors, it’s necessary to use references in the styling and use attractive photos.

The muted colour palette was chosen to make viewers feel more connected to nature. Through the branding, a muted tint has been added to some apps to establish a tone of tranquilly, relaxation, and stability, which is exactly what Minimalist design aims for a regulated and organised relax place.

Green and yellow were chosen because it is important to remember that this is a place where people can rest, so the colour combination should be delightful and appealing to both parents and children.
