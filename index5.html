Le Secret De Jasmine Hotel

Le Secret De Jasmine Hotel





Category: Rebranding Design

Client:Generation X , Y , Z (rich people , middle people, younsters and old generation also )

Project date: 2020

Le Secret de Jasmine is a guesthouse which is arranged in Pereybere, inside 3.5 km of Grand Baie. Pereybere
Theis project is about the rebranding of the hotel by designing the logo and further collaterals.



LOGO

The logo was symbolic having a flower of jasmin and had been design in a lettermark form with its name. Its written S and J with the plant of jasmin around the letters which convey the message to discover the secret of jasmin. complete meaning in one name.

As my labels are ecological, the flora and name are the natural elements. Therefore the color chosen is based on the floral reality that symbolizes passion, beauty and significance. In addition, Jasmin is a fragrance used in perfumes, soaps and lotions and is also used to add a heady fragrance to our customer’s jasmine tea available in the hotel.

That’s why the name ‘Le secret de Jasmin’ means the secret of jasmin, where people can experience a certain kind of environment, which they do not have in their everyday lives.

The sign was an attraction and thus the logo represented the sleek attitude of the company. It would not have been so efficient to use a traditional sign for the project as the intention would be to display the name and logo of the company easily.





THE PROBLEM

The problem was to design the logo in a new style and way but able to deliver the same message of the brand about the hotel concept but this time in a more attractive style.
After lots of research and the old logo, i finally able to build a concept of logo in a new style with a good palette which i had to bear in mind about its consistency whiledesigning for the collaterals.

SOLUTION

For the solution, i came up with the brand personality and some moodboards to be able to design the final logo and also able to design the collaterals such as business cards and others below which convey the clear message for people to be able to spend their vacation in the hotel.

THE PROMOTIONAL POSTER

The use of a promotional flyer is very significant, because it is the easiest way for the target audience to spend time at the hotel.
A promotional brochure contains all the important information on the hotel, such as services, location, prices and activities.
The advertisement poster requires a website link engagement to make more online contact with the nearby site easier.









THE PUBLICATION BOOK


Le Secret De Jasmine brand is totally dependent on photography, which is used to promote and market their property to clients through mediums such as poster and publication book.

As you can see in the final deliverables, there is a lot of imagery that features Le Secret De Jasmine surroundings, architecture, and interior items. To attract visitors, it’s necessary to use references in the styling and use attractive photos.
